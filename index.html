Hi, I'm Victoria!

I'm a data and ccomputational journalist at Hearst Connecticut Media Group. I spend my days automating systems, building and managing unique datasets and developing both internal and external tools for the Connecticut newsroom, as well as other newsrooms across Hearst.

I enjoy building things, analyzing data and collaborating with everyone around the newsroom to output creative copy and visuals. My work has consistently turned complex data on topics like elections, demographics, economics, education and sports into engaging, interactive narratives for diverse audiences.

I graduated from the university of Maryland in May 2024 with degrees in information science and journalism and have worked with data and backend tools at The Minnesota Star Tribune, The Baltimore Sun, The Texas Tribune and The Howard Center for Investigative Journalism.

I'm comfortable working in Python, R, SQL, Javascript, PowerBI, Tableau, Datawrapper, Flourish, Carto, ArcGIS, HTML, CSS, Adobe Illustrator, and Figma. I used lots of those skills to make this website! You can check out the documentation here.

When I'm not working I enjoy playing volleyball, reading novels, trying new knitting pattersn, playing video games and cooking.
